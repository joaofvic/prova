¿Qué es PDCA?

PDCA significa, en inglés, PLANEAR - HACER - VERIFICAR - ACTUAR, o también PLAN - HACER - VERIFICAR - AJUSTAR, lo que significa "Planificar - Hacer - Verificar - Actuar" o "Planificar - Hacer - Verificar - Ajustar".

WHAT IS GTD?

Oct 02, 2019

The GTD Method is a tool that teaches you how to use time more efficiently. In good Portuguese,
puts a few more hours in your day.
The acronym GTD - “Getting Things Done” - refers to the methodology created by David Allen and taught in his work "The Art of Making It Happen."

THE KANBAN METHOD

Oct 09, 2019

The Kanban method was born in Japan with the goal of managing the supply and flow of stock materials to production lines without waste or delays, in a very visual and easy to understand way: using color cards.
