What is PDCA?

PDCA means, in English, PLAN - DO - CHECK - ACT, or also PLAN - DO - CHECK - ADJUST, which means "Plan - Do - Verify - Act", or "Plan - Do - Verify - Adjust".

WHAT IS GTD?

Oct 02, 2019

The GTD Method is a tool that teaches you how to use time more efficiently. In good Portuguese,
puts a few more hours in your day.
The acronym GTD - “Getting Things Done” - refers to the methodology created by David Allen and taught in his work "The Art of Making It Happen."
Consists of a Method Simple and effective 5 steps that will make you manage all your tasks more efficiently.
